                                                                               Tuesday  September 6  2005      WASHINGTON  DC    It has been exactly one year since a GAO legal opinion requested by Senator Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ   found that former CMS Administrator Tom Scully illegally ordered the Medicare Actuary to withhold information from Congress regarding the true cost of the Medicare prescription drug bill  The Department of Health and Human Services was ordered by the GAO to recover Scully s salary         Tom Scully hid  120 billion in costs from the American taxpayers and the GAO found he violated the law  but a year has passed and he hasn t paid back one dime of his salary   said Senator Lautenberg   The President seems to protect any aide who misleads the American people if it advances his political agenda        Tom Scully has since left his position as the head of CMS to take a lucrative position in the private sector       To view the GAO opinion presented to

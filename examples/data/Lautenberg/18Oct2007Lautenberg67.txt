     Senator Frank R  Lautenberg                                                                                                                      Press Release        of        Senator Lautenberg                                                                                Lautenberg Comments On Planned Deployment Of NJ Troops To Iraq                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Announcement marks largest deployment of NJ troops since World War II                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Contact  Press Office  202  224 3224 Thursday  October 18  2007      WASHINGTON  D C   Sen  Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  today issued the following statement upon learning that approximately 3 200 members of the New Jersey Army National Guard will be sent to Iraq for combat duty    Our military leaders have made clear time and again that our armed forces cannot sustain the current troop levels in Iraq  Active duty  Reserve and National Guard troops in New Jersey and across the nation have already seen multiple and extended tours of duty  Our military is stretched thin and more and more of the burden is unfairly falling on the Guard  leaving us vulnerable at home                         Questions or Comments

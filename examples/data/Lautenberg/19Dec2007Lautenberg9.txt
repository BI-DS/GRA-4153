     Senator Frank R  Lautenberg                                                                                                                      Press Release        of        Senator Lautenberg                                                                                Lautenberg Statement On Bush Administration s Plan To Limit Flights At Newark  JFK Airports                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           Contact  Press Office  202  224 3224 Wednesday  December 19  2007       WASHINGTON  D C   Sen  Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  issued the following statement after the Bush Administration announced plans to limit flights at Newark Liberty International and John F  Kennedy International Airport   We put the Administration on notice and they got the message  New Jersey deserves equal treatment  The Bush Administration is finally paying attention to the endless delays at Newark Airport  While this plan is only a band aid on the much bigger safety and noise problems we face in our region and across the country  its certainly a start  We will continue to make sure the FAA takes a regional approach to addressing flight delays and doesnt thrust New Yorks problems onto New Jersey                            Questions or Comments

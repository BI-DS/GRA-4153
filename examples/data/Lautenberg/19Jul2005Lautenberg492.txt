     Senator Frank R  Lautenberg                                                                                                                      Press Release        of        Senator Lautenberg                                                                                Senator Lautenberg s Statement on the Indian Prime Minister s Speech Before a Joint Session of Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               Tuesday  July 19  2005      WASHINGTON  DC    United States Senator Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  issued the following statement today after attending the speech by Indian Prime Minister Manmohan Singh before a joint session of Congress        I was impressed by Prime Minister Singh s message to Congress  and I applaud efforts to increase and strengthen cooperation between India and the United States  As the two largest democracies in the world  we have many mutual interests that will be served by closer ties between our nations                         Questions or Comments

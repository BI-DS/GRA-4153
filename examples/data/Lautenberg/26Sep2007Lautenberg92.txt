                                                                              Lautenberg  Menendez Announce  280 000 In Federal Funds For New Ferry Docks In Camden                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             Funds To Be Used For Design  Engineering of New Docks at Waterfront Ferry Terminal                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 Contact  Press Office  202  224 3224 Wednesday  September 26  2007       WASHINGTON  D C    Sen  Frank R  Lautenberg  D NJ  and Sen  Robert Menendez  D NJ  today announced that the Federal Transportation Administration has awarded the Delaware River Port Authority  280 000 in federal funds to expand ferry service at Camdens Delaware River waterfront    These funds will enable more people to commute between Camden and Philadelphia  said Sen  Lautenberg  More and more people are using our ferries to get to and from work and these new ferry docks will result in a more efficient ferry system   Commuters in Camden will hopefully benefit from enhanced ferry service to Philadelphia  said Sen  Menendez  The more viable transportation options we have the better it is for our region   The Camden waterfront is home to many attractions  including the U S S  New Jersey  Campbells Field and the Adventure Aquarium  As a major mode of transportation across the Delaware River  the ferry service carries over 200 000 commuters and tourists each year                          Questions or Comments
